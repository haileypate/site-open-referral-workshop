Welcome!

The inaugural Open Referral workshop is taking place at the California Endowment in Oakland, on July 9-10 from 8a-5p each day.

Purpose

To generate shared understanding of the needs for (and opportunities to create) accessible, interoperable, reliable and sustainable community resource directory data.

Objectives

  • learn about the perspectives and needs of different types of stakeholders in this field
  • to generate a 'minimally viable' shared vocabulary
  • to work towards alignment around vision and values
  • to articulate hypothesis to test and to identify (then prioritize) possible next steps
